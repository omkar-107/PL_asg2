About the Course

The "Introduction to Blockchain Technology" course offers a comprehensive overview of blockchain's foundational concepts, applications, and implications. Blockchain technology has revolutionized the way data is stored, secured, and transmitted across various industries. This course is designed to provide students with a solid understanding of the underlying principles of blockchain, its potential applications beyond cryptocurrencies, and its impact on various sectors.

Course Outline

  1. Introduction to Blockchain Concepts

  2. Decentralization and Consensus Mechanisms

  3. Smart Contracts and DApps

  4. Cryptocurrencies and Digital Wallets

  5. Security and Privacy in Blockchain

  6. Real-world Use Cases of Blockchain Technology
